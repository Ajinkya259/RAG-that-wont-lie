Trust score. Freshness, source quality, retrieval consistency.

Step 5 — Constrained Generation
Model only uses retrieved context. No assumptions, no outside knowledge.

Step 6 — Citation
Document, page, timestamp.

Step 7 — Hallucination Fallback

Step 8 — Continuous Evaluation
Adversarial prompts, recall testing, hallucination testing.
